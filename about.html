How it began.

One hot summer weekend in 2015, a group of Boulderites trekked to Crested Butte for a 30th birthday celebration of unparalleled adventure. There was hiking, biking, in-town coffee shop wifi stealing, and of course camp-fire grilling and smors’ing.

Around the campfire, under the starry night sky, the group of friends began reminiscing about how ridiculous life was back in their bubble of a city. The laughs kept spilling from the lips of the crew on the hike the next day, making progress towards the epic peak formidable.

Fortunately, on the car ride home, the first draft of Edition 1 of Carbs Against Hummus was scribbled onto a bunch of sticky notes found under the passenger's seat.
