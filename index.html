Carbs Against Hummus

The first edition of the unofficial Boulder expansion pack for Cards Against Humanity.

Download Edition 1.0
  1. Click on the carbs to download the deck in a pdf
  2. Print at home or find a CU student in Norlin library with a Buff Card and ask nicely if they can print for you. (The cards print on 8.5” by 11”)
  3. Cut along the dotted line.
  4. Add to your Cards Against Humanity deck and invite a bunch of friends over.
